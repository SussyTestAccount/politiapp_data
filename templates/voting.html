Eligibility
You must be a US citizen and over the age of 18 by Election Day to vote!
3 / 4
Register
To vote, register online at vote.org or by sending your name, address and birth date to a local election office (shown on the voting map) 11 days before election day.
4 / 4
Ballots
After registration in Utah, a ballot will be sent out for you via mail. Fill in the circles for the candidate you support. Then mail it to one of Utah's voting centers
